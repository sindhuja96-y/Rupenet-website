FTTS (Financial Transaction Turbo System) is Rupenet’s advanced platform for API integration, enabling real-time data synchronization and seamless connectivity with LMS, LOS, and CBS systems.

FTTS automates credit verification, borrower onboarding, follow-ups, and repayment processes, ensuring faster loan disbursement and higher repayment rates.

FTTS easily integrates with your existing infrastructure through low-code APIs, enabling fast deployment without operational disruption.
